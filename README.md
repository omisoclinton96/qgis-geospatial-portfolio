## 📌 Featured Projects

### 1. Health Facility Mapping and Accessibility Analysis — Kenya
Mapped health facilities across multiple regions to assess service coverage and identify underserved areas. Used buffer analysis and spatial joins to evaluate geographic accessibility.

### 2. Geospatial Tracking of Participants — OCV Trial
Developed spatial visualization workflows to map participant distribution and support field coordination in a clinical trial setting.

### 3. Spatial Hotspot Analysis for Disease Risk Identification
Applied density mapping and clustering techniques to identify high-risk geographic zones and support targeted interventions.

### 4. Geospatial Visualization of Field Operations and Sample Flow
Mapped operational workflows and sample movement across facilities to improve coordination and identify bottlenecks.

## 🌍 How to Publish This Portfolio (GitHub Pages)

Follow these steps to make your portfolio live:

### 1. Create a Repository
- Go to GitHub and create a new repository
- Name it: `qgis-geospatial-portfolio`
- Set it to **Public**

---

### 2. Upload Files
- Upload all files inside your portfolio folder:
  - `index.html`
  - `README.md`
  - `assets/` folder

---

### 3. Enable GitHub Pages
- Go to **Settings**
- Click **Pages**
- Under **Source**, select:
  - **Deploy from a branch**
  - Branch: `main`
  - Folder: `/root`
- Click **Save**

---

### 4. Access Your Website
After a few seconds, your site will be live at:

https://yourusername.github.io/qgis-geospatial-portfolio/
