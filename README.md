## 📌 Project Highlights

### 🗺️ Health Facility Mapping and Accessibility Analysis — Kenya
Mapped health facilities across multiple counties to assess service coverage and geographic accessibility.

- Integrated shapefiles with facility datasets  
- Performed buffer analysis to estimate service reach  
- Identified underserved regions for targeted intervention  

**Impact:** Supported evidence-based planning and resource allocation in healthcare systems.

---

### 📍 Geospatial Tracking of Participants — OCV Trial
Developed spatial workflows to track participant distribution and support field coordination in a clinical trial.

- Visualized participant density and clustering  
- Linked spatial data with operational datasets  
- Improved follow-up planning using geographic insights  

**Impact:** Enhanced field efficiency and participant tracking strategies.

---

### 🔥 Spatial Hotspot Analysis for Risk Identification
Conducted spatial analysis to detect high-risk geographic zones using clustering and density mapping techniques.

- Generated heatmaps and hotspot visualizations  
- Compared spatial patterns across regions  
- Highlighted priority areas for intervention  

**Impact:** Enabled targeted response strategies and improved monitoring.

---

### 🔄 Geospatial Visualization of Field Operations & Sample Flow
Mapped operational workflows and sample movement across facilities to improve coordination and identify bottlenecks.

- Visualized movement between field and lab locations  
- Identified delays and inefficiencies in workflows  
- Supported operational decision-making  

**Impact:** Improved visibility and efficiency in research operations.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **GIS:** QGIS (Spatial Analysis, Mapping, CRS)
- **Programming:** R (sf, ggplot2, leaflet)
- **Data:** Shapefiles, GPS data, spatial datasets
- **Techniques:** Buffer analysis, clustering, heatmaps, spatial joins
